fill.js
Basic Usage: will fill to parent 
$('.selector').fill();
This will fill to selector's size: 
$('.selector').fill({parent: $(ele)});
To ignore width: 
$('.selector').fill({x: false});
To ignore height: 
$('.selector').fill({y: false});
Center on window: 
$('#selector').fill({center: true});
Demo:
$('.fillTest').fill();
